    Main Sponsors

    Health Right Now

    Health Right Now is a non-profit organization that is focused on using artifical intelligence to solve a lot of different public health issues. One example of the reach that this non-profit has created is when Prayag Shankar created a fundraiser to help the homeless population recover from the deadly pandemic. Prayag raised enough money to donate 900 diapers, 125 pairs of socks, 40 reusable water bottles, 30 coloring books, and lots of packages of items that contain lots of essential living utilities.

    Community Financial

    Commmunity Financial is a financial institution. They have donated $250 and 3 IPads to Student Information Technology Services. Their generous donations keep us going and we are beyond thankful for the additional hardware and money. At Plymouth-Canton Educational Park, Community Financial offers a safe place for students to earn interest on their money. This sets our students up for a better future in the real world and prepares them for a life of financial stability.
